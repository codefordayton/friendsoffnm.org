Ways to support

Financial Support

You can donate once or become a sustainer by marking your donation as "recurring - yearly"

US supporters who donate through this Friends site can deduct their donation from their taxes, and when you donate, you'll automatically get a receipt for that purpose.

Donor Perks!

$25 - $50 entitles you to one free admission to the Museum in London

$50 or more entitles you to one free admission plus 10% discount at the Museum Shop, in person or online!

$100 or more includes both the above perks plus a page in the Museum's "Books of Honor."

$1,500 or more includes recognition on the Wall of Honor in the museum in London!

Donate Now

Donate securely through Zeffy, the donor platform that delivers 100% of your donation to the nonprofit. Optional additional contributions help cover transaction fees and keep Zeffy going.

Tap into Museum News

Subscribe to the Museum's FREE newsletter, emailed directly to you once a month. You'll stay tuned in to Museum happenings, including live webinars from London for subscribers.
