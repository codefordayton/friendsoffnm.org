About Us

Your yearly membership dues contribute directly to the activities of the museum, and entitle you to:

  • A subscription to the Florence Nightingale Museum Newsletter (emailed)
  • Notice of events - virtual and in person - at the museum
  • Free admission to the Florence Nightingale Museum in London
  • 10% off purchases in the Museum Gift Shop -including online purchases

Membership Levels

Supporter level: $30 annual donation

Honor Book Supporter level: $150 or more

Friends of the Florence Nightingale Museum is a separate legal entity from the Florence Nightingale Museum in London, UK. We are established as a not-for-profit corporation under Chapter 1702 of the Ohio Revised Code, and we have a 501(c)3 status per the United States Internal Revenue Service. This allows US taxpayers to deduct contributions to Friends of the Florence Nightingale Museum on their federal tax returns. You can read our privacy policy here.
